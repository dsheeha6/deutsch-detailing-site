Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our detailing services

What areas do you service?

We currently serve Kennesaw, Acworth, Marietta, Woodstock, and Atlanta, GA. If you're nearby but unsure, feel free to contact us to confirm availability.

Do I need to provide water or electricity?

Yes, we may need access to water and a standard electrical outlet depending on the location and service. We'll let you know exactly what's needed when booking.

How long does a full detail take?

A full detail typically takes between 3 to 4 hours depending on the size and condition of the vehicle.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, credit/debit cards, Venmo, and Cash App. Payment is collected after the service is complete and you're fully satisfied.

How often should I detail my car?

We recommend a full detail every 3–6 months depending on usage, climate, and vehicle condition.

Do you offer polishing services?

Yes! We provide professional paint polishing to remove swirls, oxidation, and minor scratches—restoring depth and clarity to your paint.

Can I book online?

Absolutely. You can message us through Instagram, call, or email to set up your appointment. Online booking coming soon!
