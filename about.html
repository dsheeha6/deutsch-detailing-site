About Us

Our passion for German engineering meets professional detailing

About Deutsch Detailing

Deutsch Detailing is a premier mobile detailing service based in Acworth, GA. We bring our expertise and professional equipment directly to your location, ensuring your vehicle receives the highest quality care without the inconvenience of leaving your home or office.

What's in the Name?

The name "Deutsch" reflects our deep appreciation for German automotive engineering and the precision that comes with it. Just as German cars are known for their exceptional quality and attention to detail, we bring that same level of precision and excellence to every vehicle we service.

Meet the Founders: Danny & Alan

We're Danny and Alan, two car enthusiasts and college students at Kennesaw State University with a passion for all things automotive. We created Deutsch Detailing not just as a business, but as a way to work on and care for the cars we love. What started as a shared hobby quickly grew into a professional service, where we combine our love for cars with a commitment to providing top-tier detailing services.

Why Choose Us?

Mobile Service

We come to you, saving you time and hassle. Our fully equipped mobile detailing van brings the shop to your doorstep.

Our team consists of certified professionals with extensive experience in automotive detailing and German vehicles.
